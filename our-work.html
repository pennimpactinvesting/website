What We Do

Fellowship

Our Fellows work with funds of all sizes on on various impact investing projects throughout the semester and present findings to the managers of these funds. These projects range from sourcing, diligence, and portfolio company management, where we work to provide evidence backed recommendations for our partners. This past semester, we worked with the Lightsmith Group.

Newsletter

Our bi-weekly newsletter highlights impact investing in the news and job openings, with first access to speaker events and future project announcements and application opportunities. Fill out this form to sign up!

Events

We organize events with professionals in the impact investing space, recruiters from impact investing firms, as well as club socials to connect Penn students with interest-aligned professionals and peers.
