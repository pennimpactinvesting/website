What is impact investing?

From the Global Impact Investing Network:

Impact investments are investments made with the intention to generate positive, measurable social and environmental impact alongside a financial return. Impact investments can be made in both emerging and developed markets , and target a range of returns from below market to market rate, depending on investors' strategic goals.
